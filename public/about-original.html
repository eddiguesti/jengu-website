Our Vision

Our Story

How Jengu Was Born

Founded by a team of AI engineers and hospitality experts, Jengu emerged from a simple observation: tourism businesses spend countless hours on repetitive tasks that could be automated, preventing them from focusing on what truly matters—their guests.

After years of building AI systems for Fortune 500 companies, we saw an opportunity to bring enterprise-level automation to the tourism industry. We realized that hotels, resorts, campsites, and travel agencies were drowning in emails, struggling with outdated booking systems, and missing revenue opportunities due to manual processes.

Today, we build custom AI chatbots, phone bots, email automation systems, dynamic pricing engines, and complete API integrations that connect your entire tech stack. Every solution is tailored to your specific business needs, ensuring you get exactly what you need—nothing more, nothing less.
