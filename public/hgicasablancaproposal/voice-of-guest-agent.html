New 2★ Booking review just came in flagging F&B:

«Breakfast was not great – coffee machine broken, nothing left by 9am.»Booking · 2026-05-12 · German guest · intensity 0.78

This isn't isolated. F&B complaints have appeared 12 of the last 12 months. Top recurring sub-issues:

  • Limited variety – mentioned in 8/12 months
  • Buffet empty / runs out – 5/12 months
  • Broken coffee machine – 4/12 months
  • Slow service – 3/12 months

Please reply to this email in one line: what action you took. The agent will verify in 4 weeks whether complaints in this sub-category stopped.
