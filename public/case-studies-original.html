The Challenge

Managing 8 campsites across the South of France, Campasuns faced significant operational inefficiencies that were holding back growth and profitability.

Communication Bottlenecks

Manual email handling led to delayed responses, inconsistent communication quality, and overwhelmed staff during peak seasons

Static Pricing Limitations

Fixed pricing models couldn't adapt to market fluctuations, competitor changes, weather patterns, or local events—leaving revenue on the table

Operational Inconsistencies

Varying processes across different campsite locations created inefficiencies and made scaling difficult

Our Solution

We implemented a comprehensive automation and optimization strategy that addressed each challenge systematically:

Process Mapping & Optimization

Used Miro to map out all business processes across campsites, identifying inefficiencies and standardizing best practices

AI Email Automation

Developed custom Python application integrated with OpenAI API to automatically handle, categorize, and respond to customer emails 24/7

Operational Systems Automation

Automated repetitive administrative tasks and created consistent workflows across all 8 campsite locations

Custom Dynamic Pricing Software

Built the industry's first AI-powered dynamic pricing engine specifically designed for campsites—monitoring competitors, weather, events, and historical data to optimize revenue

Dynamic Pricing Innovation

The crown jewel of this transformation was our custom-built dynamic pricing platform—a sophisticated ML system that doesn't exist anywhere else in the campsite industry. Traditional pricing tools couldn't handle the unique complexities of campsite management, so we built one from the ground up.

Machine Learning Predictions

Inside the Platform

Explore the AI-powered features that make intelligent campsite pricing possible

Competitor Discovery

Automatically discover and track competitor campsites within a customizable radius using camping-and-co.com data. The system monitors 10+ competitors in real-time, tracking their pricing strategies and availability to inform intelligent pricing decisions.

Multi-Source Data Enrichment

The platform enriches historical booking data with weather information (temperature, precipitation, sunshine hours), holiday calendars, school breaks, and temporal features (day of week, season, weekend indicators). All data sources are automatically synchronized and processed for ML analysis.

Strategic Pricing Controls

Choose from Conservative, Balanced, or Aggressive pricing strategies with fine-tuned controls for demand sensitivity (30%-90%), price aggression (40%-100%), and occupancy targets (65%-90%). Each strategy adapts dynamically based on forecasted demand and market conditions.

ML-Powered Price Optimization

Advanced machine learning algorithms analyze demand forecasts, competitor pricing, weather patterns, and historical correlations to predict optimal prices up to 60 days ahead. The visualization shows current vs. optimized pricing overlaid with demand forecasts, enabling data-driven pricing decisions.

Revenue & Occupancy Forecasting

Dual forecasting charts compare current revenue/occupancy projections against ML-optimized predictions. The system provides 14-day forecasts showing potential revenue uplift and occupancy improvements, helping managers make informed capacity and pricing decisions.
