<h2>Understanding Process Mapping and Its Importance</h2>

<p>At the heart of effective business automation lies <strong>process mapping</strong>. Process mapping is the practice of creating a visual representation of the workflow within an organization. It involves identifying all the steps in a process, the people involved, and the information exchanged. This visualization helps businesses understand how their processes function, where inefficiencies may lie, and how they can be improved.</p>

<p>In the UK, where businesses operate in a highly competitive and regulated environment, process mapping has become an indispensable tool.
